My Journey Into Software Development

My journey into software development started with a simple curiosity: how do websites and applications actually work behind the screen? That curiosity slowly turned into a habit of experimenting, breaking things, fixing them, and learning one concept at a time.

As a Computer Science student, I began by strengthening my programming fundamentals and problem solving through C++ and competitive programming. That foundation helped me think more clearly about logic, efficiency, and structure before moving deeper into real-world development.

Over time, I started building full-stack projects using React, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and SQL databases. Working on these projects taught me how frontend experiences, backend APIs, databases, authentication, and deployment all connect to create something useful.

I enjoy learning by building. Every project, bug, hackathon, and new technology gives me a better understanding of how to write cleaner code, design better user flows, and solve problems with more confidence.
